The Story Behind
Better Nights

Better Nights began with a simple observation: college nightlife is fundamentally disconnected.

Created by UO student William Cleaves as part of the Oregon Innovation Challenge, the platform was born out of frustration. Students were constantly struggling to find the right environment to meet each other and connect on weekends. People were stepping into parties that didn't match their energy, or worse, wandering off-campus with no clear direction.

Connection Meets Safety

But the problem went far deeper than just finding a good time. William noticed a recurring, dangerous trend: friends having trouble getting home safely at night.

Whether it was blocked routes, sketchy encounters, or just the anxiety of walking alone in the dark, the current tools weren't enough. Better Nights was built to bridge that gap. By combining real-time campus activity with an uncompromising safety suite, we give students the power to own their night.

100% Private, Always.

We believe safety shouldn't require trading away your personal data. That's why Better Nights is built with a foundation of absolute privacy.

Your location data is never sold, tracked, or stockpiled. When you trigger an SOS, only the trusted friends you explicitly select get your live coordinates. Those you don't want to have it, simply don't get it. You are always in control of your footprint.
