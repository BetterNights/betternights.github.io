Built for the weekend.

Planning a night out shouldn't feel like a logistical nightmare. Better Nights gets you out of the group chat and into the city. It is a dedicated platform designed to help you find where the energy is, meet up with friends, and actually enjoy your evening.

Know before you go.

Whether you want a crowded venue or a quiet place to grab a drink, our active map shows you exactly what is happening in real time. No more guessing where to head next. Your group gets the right information to make a decision and keep moving.

Safety on demand.

Things change fast on a night out, and people get separated. Standard location apps force you to constantly broadcast your whereabouts. Better Nights takes a completely different approach.

Our SOS feature lets you drop your exact coordinates to a trusted circle of friends, but only when you actually need to. If someone wanders off, finding them is fast and accurate.

Privacy by design.

Your location data belongs to you. We built this app to keep you connected and safe without tracking your every move. When you decide the night is over, the app stops sharing.
