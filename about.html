Made for the night.
Built for you.

College nightlife is fundamentally broken. We decided it was time to fix it.

Better Nights began as a project for the Oregon Innovation Challenge. Our founder, UO student William Cleaves, noticed a glaring disconnect on campus. Weekends were supposed to be about letting go, but instead, they were often defined by friction. Students were wandering the streets looking for the right crowd, stepping into spaces that didn't match their energy, or worse—struggling to find a safe way home.

We realized that existing social apps were great at keeping people on their screens, but terrible at bringing them together in the real world. So, we started building.

Find your people. instantly.

Finding the right room shouldn't be a guessing game. Your Friday night deserves better than aimless walking.

We designed Better Nights to seamlessly connect you with the environments you actually want to be in. Whether you're looking for a massive dance floor, a low-key kickback, or just a place to meet new people, the platform cuts out the noise. It’s about putting you exactly where you want to be, with the people who match your vibe.

Your safety. In your hands.

The walk home matters just as much as the destination. As we talked to more students, the same story kept coming up: friends feeling anxious, unsafe, or lost while navigating off-campus areas in the dark.

We knew that simply finding a party wasn't enough. We had to build a platform that actually protected you. That’s why we integrated a live map driven by real-time campus reporting. Blocked routes, sketchy encounters, active incidents—you see it all before you step foot out the door. And if things go wrong, an uncompromising SOS system alerts your closest friends instantly.

Privacy. It’s a right.

We believe your personal safety shouldn't cost you your personal data. Period.

Better Nights is built on a foundation of absolute, uncompromising privacy. We don't sell your location. We don't stockpile your footprints. When you trigger an alert, your exact coordinates are sent exclusively to the circle of friends you explicitly trust. Those you don't want to have it, simply don't get it.

We built Better Nights so you can focus on the music, the people, and the moment. Leave the rest to us.
